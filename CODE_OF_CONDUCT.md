# Code Of Conduct

Gurubodh contributors are expected to work with care, patience, and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Keep feedback specific, constructive, and connected to the work.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clarity when needed.
- Welcome questions from new contributors.
- Protect private information and project-sensitive material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private communication or sensitive project information without permission.
- Repeatedly disrupting reviews, issues, or discussions.
- Ignoring maintainer direction about repository safety, scope, or conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close issues or pull requests, remove content, or restrict repository access when behavior harms the project or contributors.

Concerns should be reported directly to the Gurubodh maintainers through the team's private communication channel.
